The portal requires the employee ID and an approved verification factor. If self-service verification fails, the user must contact the Service Desk from a known phone number; the technician verifies identity before issuing a temporary password. Temporary passwords expire after 30 minutes and must be changed at first sign-in. Support staff never ask users to send an existing password by email, chat, or ticket.
